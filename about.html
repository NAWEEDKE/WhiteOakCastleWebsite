ABOUT THE VENUE

WHITE OAK CASTLE

Blending timeless Victorian elegance with modern amenities to create Kerala’s premier destination for milestone events.

Architectural Grandeur & Natural Beauty

Nestled in the vibrant heart of Moodal near Kuttippuram, Kerala, White Oak Castle stands as an architectural masterpiece where timeless grandeur meets modern sophistication. Living up to our philosophy, "Where Royalty Meets Celebrations," this premium convention center captivates guests with its striking white Victorian facade, soaring columns, and meticulously manicured gardens.

Designed to cater to grand weddings, professional photoshoots, and massive conventions, White Oak Castle offers a seamless blend of luxurious indoor spaces and lush, picturesque outdoor settings. Our commitment to excellence ensures that every milestone event is executed with flawless elegance and comfort.
