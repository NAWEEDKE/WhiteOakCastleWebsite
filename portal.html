Terms & Conditions

1. Holding of Date & Deposits

To secure your date at White Oak Castle, a non-refundable deposit is required at the time of booking. The specific deposit amount varies based on the event type. Your reservation is considered "Pending" until the deposit is received and confirmed by our team. Once confirmed, the date is officially held for your exclusive use.

2. Payments & Balance

The remaining balance of the venue rental fee must be paid in full at least 14 days prior to your scheduled event. Failure to pay the balance by this deadline may result in the cancellation of your booking and forfeiture of your deposit.

3. Cancellations & Refunds

As stated, the initial booking deposit is strictly non-refundable. If you cancel your event within 30 days of the scheduled date, any additional payments made toward the balance are also non-refundable. Cancellations made more than 30 days in advance may be eligible for a partial refund of the balance (excluding the deposit), subject to management approval.

4. Damages & Liability

The client is responsible for any damage to the White Oak Castle property, structural art, or facilities caused by the client, their guests, or contracted vendors during the event. An additional security deposit may be required prior to the event, which will be fully refunded within 7 days post-event if no damages occur.

5. Privacy Policy

By registering, you consent to our collection and storage of your contact information for the purpose of managing your booking. We will not sell or share your personal data with third parties.
