Kerala's Premier Event Space

The White Oak Castle welcomes you with a grand air-conditioned foyer, white steel structures, and striking olive green accents. Nestled in Moodal near Kuttippuram, our space connects indoor luxury with the lush outdoor greenery of Malappuram.

We specialize in hosting grand weddings, nikahs, receptions, engagement ceremonies, and graduation days, offering a capacity of minimum of 1500 guests in absolute comfort.
